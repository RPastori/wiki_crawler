Western[edit]

  • West Coast hip hop
  • Bakersfield Hip Hop - Originally from Bakersfield also referred to as: Central California Hip hop, San Joaquin Valley Hip Hop, Central Valley Hip hop, or Kern County Hip Hop.
  • Central California Hip Hop - Originally from the San Joaquin Valley and the Central Coast from the cities of Bakersfield, Fresno, |Modesto, Stockton, Ventura, Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, Monterey, San Benito, and Santa Cruz.
  • Central Valley Hip Hop - Originally from Sacramento County, Butte County, Colusa County, Glenn County, El Dorado County, Fresno County, Kings County, Madera County, Merced County, Placer County, San Joaquin County, Shasta County, Stanislaus County, Sutter County, Tehama County, Tulare County, Yuba County, Yolo County, and Kern County.
    • Chicano rap - originally from East Los Angeles, California
    • Gangsta rap
    • G-funk - originally from Los Angeles, California
    • Mobb music & Hyphy - originally from the San Francisco Bay Area in California
    • Jerkin' - originally from Los Angeles
    • Native American hip hop
    • Northwest hip hop - originally from Oregon or Washington
    • Ratchet - originally from Los Angeles, California
    • Electro Hop - originally from Los Angeles, California
