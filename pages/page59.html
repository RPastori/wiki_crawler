Bands disbanded[edit]

  • Amoral[1]
  • Black Sabbath[2]

Events[edit]

  • On February 2 and 4, Black Sabbath played their final two shows at the Genting Arena in Birmingham. These dates were the final two dates of the band's farewell tour The End.[3][4]
  • Aerosmith will embark on their final tour after 47 years together, titled the Aero-Vederci Baby! tour.[5]
  • Canadian festival Heavy Montreal will take a year off, returning in July 2018.[6]

Deaths[edit]

  • January 28 – Geoff Nicholls, former keyboardist of Black Sabbath, died from lung cancer at the age of 68.[7]
  • February 13 – Trish Doan, bassist of Kittie, died from undisclosed reasons at the age of 31.[8]
  • February 14 – Tony Särkkä, former multi-instrumentalist of Abruptum, Ophthalamia, Vondur and War, died from undisclosed reasons at the age of 45.[9]
